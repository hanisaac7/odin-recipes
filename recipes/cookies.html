Cookies

Description

Cookies are a sweet dessert that is the perfect end to a wonderful day. These pastries are very sweet, but extremely delicious and super easy to make! All that is required is only a couple ingredients and an oven.

I enjoy baking cookies with my wife, I have a lot of fun doing it with her.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix all dry ingredients
  2. Add in wet ingredients
  3. Create dough by mixing
  4. Shape and bake
  5. Serve and enjoy
