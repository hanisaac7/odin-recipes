Tacos

Description

Tacos are a staple Mexican dish. They are a simple yet tasty food that could be made from the comforts of your home. As long as you have a tortilla and some meat, everything else can be as flexible as you want.

I enjoy eating tacos at local street stands.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Choice of meat (beef or pork)
  • Onion
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Warm tortilla on pan
  2. Cook meat
  3. Place meat on tortilla
  4. Add onion and cilantro
  5. Serve and enjoy
