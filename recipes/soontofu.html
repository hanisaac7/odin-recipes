Soontofu

Description

Soontofu is a Korean comfort food dish with a spicy broth that is complemented by tofu, meat and other vegetables. It is typically served on a black stone pot and served with a side of rice. Many people enjoy having a separate dish of galbi to add to the experience.

I personally enjoy soontofu and it is one of my favorite dishes to eat especially around Orange County.

Ingredients

  • Soft Tofu
  • Choice of meat (beef or pork)
  • Soup broth
  • Green onion

Steps

  1. Boil soup broth
  2. Add choice of meat
  3. Boil until fully cooked
  4. Add green onion
  5. Serve and enjoy
